Amsterdam Bos

The amsterdamse Bos is an English park or landscape park in the muncipalities of Amstelveen and Amsterdam. The park was built in 1930s,mostly by the unemployement relief. Annually,almost 4.5 million people visit this park, which has a size of 1,000 hectares and is approximately three times the size of central park in New York City. Inside,one can find animal parks, several sporting clubs, rowing course,gardens and remembrance monuments. There is also a fum forest inside the park where you can move through trees on ropes,ladders, bridges,zip lines and various different climbing elements. The high rope adventure park has nine courses ranging from 4 to 15 meters high.The courses have various levels of difficulty which makes the park suitable for young and old.

Things to do in Amsterdamse Bos

  • Museum Tram

    Arrive in style with the Museum Tram. The Museum Tram departs from haarlemmermeer station and takes you all the way to the Amsterdamse Bos.You can get off at the forest entrance, but also contineue a few stops. it's a fun way to travel to the forests

  • Bos Theather

    Picture yourself snuggled up in a blanket, enjoying a special theatrical performance in the middle of a forest.The surroundings are nothing short of stunning,and the decor is amazing

The Neighbourhood Loomer

Loomer is a neighbourhood of amsterdam,netherlands.It is bordered by the staatsliedenbuurt on the south and the Spaarndammerbuurt on the west. It is a non-residental area,containing the park by the same name. It is also a former borough of the city,which was merged with into the borough of Amsterdam-West.
